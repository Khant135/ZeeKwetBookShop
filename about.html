About Us

We are the organization that sell the books across the Myanmar. We start this business at the start of the year 2010 and now it is about 9 years that we had been selling the books. The first book shop is located in Yangon and it still exists as the main store of the organization. Now we have about 5 branches within the Yangon city and other 5 branches across the Myanmar. Now we are relasing the website that the customer can order the books from home without coming to bookshop. Right Now, the website can be used across the country but the delivery service is only available within Yangon City. The others places are going to be added in the future.

In our Book Store, a wide varity of books can be bought. We also have many categories for the customer to choose. Categories like Action, Adventure, Sci-Fiction, Romance and Thriller are very popular among the reader. We have ablut 40K of books in the store therfore the customer can get the books without delaying or waiting for too long. The price for the books are also not too expensive and we also have the special discounts for the customer who buys a quite lots of books at one time. And also we held an event once a year and in that event we sell the books by reducing the original price. We also held the game show and competition in those events.
